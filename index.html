About

Want To Know About Us ??

  • 2017-2018

    Exploring The World Of NGO's

    In 2017-2018 i.e the very first year of graduation we got some beautiful hearted and minded mentors one of whom was associated to an NGO named Srishti. With the passing on time and building connections we also got a chance to get into it where we learnt about the duties, programmes, strategies and aids of a NGO

  • 2018-2019

    An Idea is Born

    Our team has participated in a Hackathon. There was a problem statement on waste management and management of garbage trucks. We chose that problem , analyse the situation and start our reasearch more on it as we were already having some ideas on that. We created our first prototype of idea. Our solution on the problem statement was appreciated by the jury at that time.

  • 2019-2020

    Our First Phase

    After developing the prototype we started researching about the idea and had a wish of launching it and properly working on it. So after gaining knowledge we started our 1st phase of development and built this website
